Project: Customer Behavior & Business Performance Analytics Dashboard

I developed an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution in Power BI to analyze customer behavior, branch performance, customer retention, loyalty programs, and revenue trends. The objective was to provide management with a centralized reporting platform for monitoring key business KPIs and making data-driven decisions.

The project involved data collection from multiple business entities, including customers, visits, memberships, branches, services, and feedback data. I performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query, designed a star schema data model, and established relationships between fact and dimension tables.

I developed advanced DAX measures for revenue analysis, customer acquisition, repeat customer tracking, churn analysis, cohort retention analysis, membership performance, branch ranking, and price impact analysis. Interactive dashboards were created for Executive Overview, Branch Performance, Customer 360, Membership & Loyalty, and Price Impact Analysis.

The solution enabled stakeholders to track customer lifecycle metrics, identify high-performing branches, monitor retention trends, evaluate loyalty program effectiveness, and analyze the business impact of pricing changes. The dashboards reduced manual reporting effort and improved visibility into overall business performance through interactive and automated reporting.
